# Agile Ceremonies and QA Role

## 1. Backlog Refinement
QA Responsibilities:
- Review acceptance criteria for clarity and testability
- Identify edge cases and negative scenarios
- Raise questions on ambiguous requirements
- Suggest risk-based prioritization

## 2. Sprint Planning
QA Responsibilities:
- Estimate testing effort
- Identify regression impact
- Confirm test data requirements
- Align automation scope (if applicable)

## 3. Daily Standup
QA Responsibilities:
- Report testing progress
- Highlight blockers
- Raise defect trends or environment issues
- Escalate SLA risk if needed

## 4. Sprint Review
QA Responsibilities:
- Confirm acceptance criteria validation
- Present defect summary
- Highlight open critical issues
- Confirm regression coverage

## 5. Sprint Retrospective
QA Responsibilities:
- Share defect aging analysis
- Identify root cause patterns
- Suggest process improvements
- Recommend preventive measures
